One Dollar Back To School Foundation

ODBS FOUNADATION

The One Back To School Foundation (ODBS) is a non-profitable organization focused on improving education, leadership, environment and community volunteerism. We are committed to mentoring young children and youth for a better future for our society.

Donate

About us

The One Dollar Back To School project was established and founded in 2019 by Mr. Barasa Wycliffe after carrying out one dollar (100 shillings) campaign in a local primary school in Busia, Kenya. He was able to contribute and pay for students exam fees and thus brought rise of the non-profit organisation after seeing the impact of his contributions to the students

Sponsor a Child

Create a personal bond and change a life by sponsoring a pupil or student. Child sponsorship is just a $1 a week with One Dollar Back to School Foundation.

Sponsor a school

Build a personal bond by bringing transformation in school.You can support a school for renovation, library, teachers and tree planting in both primary and secondary and other institutions.

Campaign with us

Discover how and join us to campaign for a future free from poverty. One Dollar Back to School welcomes you to join and help in advertisement, giving and donating.

Volunteer with us

Our volunteers are the heart of The One Dollar Back to School Foundation. Without them, we would not be able to continue to support the thousands of students and pupil who rely on us for information and support for their education.
